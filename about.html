Celebrating 35 years
of real friendship and connections on Von OPA

It is all about where we come from! This group is made to colour up our life! Color up with: food from our ancestors, the culture, the traditions, music, languages, and movies, favourite places, arts… all what make our life more colourful and our hearts warm! So let us share what we learned from our ancestors and what has shaped our lives and what we love! We want to create an explosion of colours and diversity! All together without prejudices but with the love to learn more about other cultures! Feel free to share what you like and bring your inspiration and experiences from your ancestors to the group. Let us then organise something together!

The origin of the group was the idea to cook together but culture, art & new languages includes so much more! The goal is to bring together great people; it should not be business so please only report if you agree to our sharing concept. We would like to cultivate a friendly and respectful relationship! Myself Sun :D I am influenced by my late Grand Pa – An early 20th century chef belongs to South of India; the gods own country - Kerala (the origin of Ayurveda). I can share amazing tips & tricks of Indian kitchen, beautiful Indian movies, photography tricks, space for organising our events etc. So that’s me, so what about you?
